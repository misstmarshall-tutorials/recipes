Ingredients:
- 2 large eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
- 1 cup of baby spinach shredded
- 1/2 sweet onion diced
- 1/2 bell pepper diced
- 1 cup sharp cheese shredded.
- 1 tablespoon of 2% milk
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
 1. Preheat skillet to medium high
 2. In medium bowl, whisk eggs and stir in salt, pepper, and milk
 3. Heat oil until hot.
 4. Sautee onions, bell peppers, and spinach.
 5. Pour eggs and cook scrambled.
 6. Sprinkle cheese until it begins to melt.
 7. Serve while it's still warm
